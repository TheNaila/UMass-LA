High Quality Feedback

Compliments are nice to have and they will boost confidence but they won't help you grow and reach the next level
Ask your manager/supervisor for at least one skill that they would like to see you improve

Managing Perception

As people we like to pass judgements but as a candidate someone's judgement of you might dictate whether or not you become employed. You can't stop people from judging but you can anticpate what those judgements might be and turn them into something that depicts you in a positive light

Conversational Goals

At the beginning of every important conversation you have, you should clearly state the objective and the outcome. That way you make it easier for the person you are addressing to follow along and tailor their thoughts/advice accordingly. Also highlight whats in this conversation for them
